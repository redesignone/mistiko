Shhh... It's a Secret...

Once upon a time in Ancient Greece, there was a coffee shop at the foot of the Acropolis. Many people came to this coffee shop, enjoying it so much, they didn't want to share it, so they spoke of it to no one. It was their secret place, and the coffee shop soon came to be called Mistiko..
